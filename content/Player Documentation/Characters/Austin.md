# Abilities
- Long Limbs: You can reach 10' for melee attacks
- Sneak Attack Lv 1: When you have advantage on an attack, deal an additional 2d6 damage. Deal an additional 1d6 damage per level of sneak attack.
-  Extra Attack Lv 1: You can attack 1x per level per turn. Gain +1 per level to the damage of the attack. Max Level 3
- Rage Lv 1: On your turn, you can enter a rage as a bonus action. While raging, you have advantage on Str checks and saves. You have resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage. When you make a melee attack using Str, you gain a bonus 1D6+1 to the damage roll. Your rage lasts up to 1 minute, or until you do not make an attack or take damage on your turn.
